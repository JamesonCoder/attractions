Children's Fairyland

If you have children, Fairyland is a great attraction to bring them to so they can see the animals, enjoy the rides, and experience all the shows that the park offers.

Rides include the Jolly Trolly, a kid-sized train that goes through a tunnel! Another ride is called the Wonder-Go-Round, a carousel that incorporates characters from Alice in Wonderland. One of the more famous rides include a ferris wheel called Anansi's Magic Web.

There are also daily Puppet Shows for you and your children to enjoy, as well as Weekend Performers who put on a show every weekend.
