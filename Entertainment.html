Entertainment

Jack London Regal Cinema

The Regal Cinema at Jack London Square is one of the best places to go to catch a movie with friends after a meal, a walk nearby Jack London, after school or work, or for whenever. With movie tickets at six dollars on Tuesdays and Sundays, it's an invitation to go see your favorite new releases.


New Parkway Theatre

Another good movie theatre location located between Broadway and Telegraph. The threatre shows many old and independent movies, as well as new releases. Come here to enjoy good food and a beer or two while watching a film.


Plank

An entertaiment area at its finest. Plank newly opened up shop a few years ago. One can go here to enjoy food while watching a game on tv, go bowling with friends and family, play arcade games, or simply just lounge around and chat it up.

Before Plank opened, the space used to be occupied by Barnes and Nobles.


Yoshi's

A popular venue and restaurant specializing in sushi. A good choice to go eat and drink while watching some of your favorite artists, singers, and comedians perform on stage.
